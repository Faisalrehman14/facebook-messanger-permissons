Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

1. Introduction

This Facebook Messenger Demo application ("App") is designed to demonstrate Facebook API permissions for Meta App Review purposes. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Data We Collect

When you connect with Facebook, we may access:

  • public_profile: Your name and profile picture
  • pages_show_list: List of Facebook Pages you manage
  • pages_read_engagement: Posts and engagement data from your selected Page
  • pages_messaging / pages_utility_messaging: Messages sent through the demo interface only

3. How We Use Data

Data is used solely to demonstrate API functionality for App Review. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Data is processed in your browser and via Facebook's Graph API — we do not store data on external servers.

4. Data Retention

We do not persistently store your Facebook data. Session tokens exist only in your browser during active use and are cleared on logout.

5. Your Rights

You may revoke app access at any time via Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites. Contact us to request deletion of any data we may hold.

6. Third-Party Services

This App uses Facebook/Meta APIs governed by Meta's Data Policy.

7. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact: your-email@example.com (replace with your email before App Review submission).
