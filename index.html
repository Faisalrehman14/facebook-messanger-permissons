Manage your Facebook Page messages in one place

PageChat Hub is a customer messaging platform for Facebook Pages. Read conversations, reply to customers, track post engagement, and send order updates — all from one dashboard.

  • Unified inbox for all your Pages
  • Reply to Messenger customers in real time
  • Post engagement & analytics
  • Order & account utility notifications

How we use Facebook permissions

public_profile

Identify you when you sign in and show your name in the dashboard.

pages_show_list

List Pages you manage so you can pick which inbox to open.

pages_messaging

Read and reply to customer messages on your Page via Messenger.

pages_read_engagement

Show post performance — likes, comments, and reach on your Page.

pages_utility_messaging

Send order confirmations, shipping updates, and account alerts to customers who opted in.
