User Data Deletion Instructions

Required by Meta Platform — Last updated: May 20, 2026

How to remove your data from PageChat Hub

PageChat Hub does not permanently store your Facebook messages on our servers. Data is loaded from Meta's API in your browser session only.

Step 1 — Revoke app access (recommended)

  1. Open Facebook → Settings & privacy → Settings
  2. Go to Apps and Websites
  3. Find PageChat Hub (or your app name) → Remove

This immediately stops PageChat Hub from accessing your profile, Pages, and messages.

Step 2 — Request deletion by email

Email us at faisalboss788@gmail.com with subject Data Deletion Request and include:

  • Your Facebook name or user ID
  • Page name (if applicable)

We will confirm deletion of any server logs within 30 days.

Step 3 — Page message history

Message history on your Facebook Page is controlled by Meta. To delete customer conversations, use Facebook Page Inbox or Meta Business Suite.
